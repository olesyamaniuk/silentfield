Gem Forest:
Gem Hunter Match 3

It's basically a chill match-3 game in this magical forest setting. There's tons of islands and gems to collect. We've all been there - you say 'just one more level' and next thing you know it's 2am and you're still going 'okay fine, ONE more.'
